How it works A temperature blanket gives every day of the year its own colour based on how warm or cold it was. Pick a place and a year — each horizontal stripe is one day, shaded from icy blues through to fiery reds. Data fetched live from Open-Meteo.
Note from the designer Temperature blankets are a beloved knitting and crochet tradition — one stripe per day, colour chosen by the thermometer. This tool generates the colour plan for any place and year so you can cast on straight away.
